Dates Cake

Home About Contact

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered jaggery
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1½ cups warm milk
  • 20 dates

Instructions

  1. Soak dates in warm milk for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder & soda, and jaggery.
  3. Add melted butter, vanilla, and the soaked dates blend.
  4. Pour batter into a greased tin.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 40–45 minutes.
