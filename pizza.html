30-Minute Pizza

Home About Contact

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 tsp sugar & salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Pizza sauce, cheese, toppings

Instructions

  1. Activate yeast in warm water with sugar.
  2. Knead with flour, salt, oil into dough.
  3. Roll out, spread sauce, add toppings.
  4. Bake at 200°C for 15–20 mins.
