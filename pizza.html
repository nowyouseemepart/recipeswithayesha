30-Minute Pizza

Home About Contact

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (veggies, paneer, chicken, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil. Knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough into a circle and place on a baking tray.
  5. Spread pizza sauce, add cheese, and toppings.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (390°F) for 15 minutes.
